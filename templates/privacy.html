Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide when you create an account (such as your name and email address) and the expense data you enter while using Spendly, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We also collect basic usage information such as login timestamps to maintain account security.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries and category breakdowns, and keeping your account secure. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the service to you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. Passwords are stored in hashed form and are never accessible in plain text. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality obligations. We do not integrate with advertising networks or data brokers.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 5 business days.
